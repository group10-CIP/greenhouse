Therapeutic Indoor Plants

by: Jerome Sayawan

In the recent events, most specifically the pandemic that happened, people have been staying at home. Due to this, there are people who tend to get bored and even some of the people in this pandemic time picked-up the hobby of taking care of plants. But little they know that these plants can be therapeutic in some ways to the people. Not just by the natural function of the plants that cleanses the air from pollutants. They also give some calming effects to the minds of people in the worrying time that now is. In this article, it will provide some of the indoor plants that has therapeutic effects to the people.


The Jasmine (Jasminum) is a white colored flower. It came from the family of olives. It can give wellness effect to the humans by its calming fragrance that can be mentally relaxing. And even in the research made by the researchers of Wheeling Jesuit University said that by having this plant near bed can help the people have a better night sleep.


The Lavender (Lavandula) is a bright purple flowering plant. It also has a benefit similar to the jasmine by providing a nice fragrance that helps the people to relax and have a good sleep in the night. In the studies made by the Miami Miller School of Medicine that bath oil made with the flowers fragrance can calm babies.


The Aloe vera is succulent plant species. It has a benefit of cleansing the air. It also has a sticky gel as a sap when you cut off one the leaves that helps cure insect bites, wounds and sunburns. And also said by people that the sap it has can help regrow or help vitalize the growth of the hair.


The Snake plants (Dracaena trifasciata) is a flowering plants that has a look of long spear shaped leaves with a yellow outline. Its main benefit is its natural ability to cleanse harmful pollutants such as carbon monoxide and nitrogen monoxide. These plant are even identified by the NASA to be a good purifying agent in their place.


The Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum) is a white flowering plant with green leaves. It has benefit of cleaning pollutants in the air made by the household appliances. They are a nice addition for decoration as well that could be fitted in a center table or similar places. Its name really capture it look it is a peaceful to look at.


The Eucalyptus shrub is a plant with green round leaves. It has a really nice benefits not only by cleaning the pollutants in the air but also its nice fragrance. It is also believed that it has the healing ability to treat asthma, colds, and congestion. It fragrance is what could be the factor to do such abilities.
