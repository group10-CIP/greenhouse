ANDREANA

Tillandsia andreana, the Tillandsia andreana has wispy bright green leaves and a round rosette shape. A rare plant, that will grow into quite an impressive clump over time. It is unique looking air plant which originates from Colombia. The andreana air plants have compact bases and delicate, thin leaves that extend in all directions. The andreana air plant is rare in cultivation and a wonderful addition for indoors especially terrariums. The Andreana needs lots of good air flow and can be more sensitive to thermal changes due to its thin leaves.
