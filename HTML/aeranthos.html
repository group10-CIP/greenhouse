AERANTHOS

Tillandsia aeranthos is an epiphyte that tends to grow alone or in small clusters. The clusters can consist of two to up to a dozen plants. You can find them from sea level to hundreds meters in Argentina, Paraguay, Uruguay and Brazil. Aeranthos flowers like clockwork every spring by producing a colorful inflorescence. It extends above the leaves and has rosy-purple floral bracts that are about 2.3-2.7 centimeters long from which about 5-20 small blue-violet flowers emerge with 3 petals that have edged tips. A small vase filled with rock or sand will allow the plant to stand out and will create a wonderful focal point to any room it lives in. Or group it together with several smaller air plants as the aeranthos can be a wonderful centerpiece with its bright and colorful bloom. Aeranthos plant is a slow-growing variety of air plant. It requires minimal care and maintenance. It only needs bright filtered light and controlled watering to prevent constant wetness.
