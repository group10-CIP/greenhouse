TEMPERATE


Temperate plants are often found in forests bearing the same name. Temperate forests are biomes with temperatures that changes all year round, which are the seasons of winter, spring, summer, and fall. As such plants that thrive here are those that are able to withstand its constant change in environment. This type of biome is usually found in the eastern regions of America, Canada, China, and Japan. Temperate plants usually include things like overgrown trees, smaller trees, shrubs, and ferns. Examples of plants that thrives in this environment are oaks, birches, walnut trees, sycamore, and elms.
