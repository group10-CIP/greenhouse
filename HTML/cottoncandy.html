COTTON CANDY / HOUSTON

Tillandsia cotton candy or Tillandsia houston is a hybrid air plant from T.stricta x T.recurvifolia which is both an air plant. The best features of both of the parent plants are displayed in the T. houston. It gets its fast growing rosette shape from the T. stricta, and its lovely silver color from the T. recurvifolia. T. houston has soft leaves that are silvery in color with purple accents when in bloom. And let's talk about those blooms! These plants emit beautiful cotton candy colored blooms with delicate white flowers. Houston air plants can grow to be quite large, and if left to grow after propagation, they can grow into pretty impressive clumps! These plants lend well to use in decorating due to their unique color, impressive blooms, and shape.
